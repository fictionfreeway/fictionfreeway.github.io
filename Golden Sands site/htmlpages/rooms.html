Enjoy Luxurious Space With Our King Rooms



Our king rooms feature a single king-size bed, sleeper sofa, table and chairs, small refrigerator, microwave oven, coffeemaker, in room safe and an iron and ironing board. These rooms have a private balcony overlooking the magnificent Atlantic Ocean.

Our Doubles are Bigger Than Most



Our double queen rooms feature 2 queen-size beds for even more comfort. In addition, they come standard with a coffeemaker, small refrigerator, microwave
oven, in room safe, iron and ironing board, table and chairs and even a sleeper sofa.

Relax in your room or out on your own private balcony.

A Perfect Solution For Families



Our split room is like a mini-suite. It is a double room with each bed in a separate space. This means the kids can go to bed while the parents are still up enjoying the evening. Like our other rooms, the split room features a microwave oven, small refrigerator, coffeemaker, in room safe, ironing board and iron, table and chairs, and a sleeper sofa.

Everyone will be comfortable! Lots of room for you and your family!

More Value for Your Money



Our double efficiency rooms allow you to get more value for your vacation dollar. A full-size refrigerator, stove, sink, and utensils allow you to prepare meals in your room and avoid the extra expense of dining out.

The double efficiencies also are spacious enough for the whole family. In the room, you will find 2 queen-size beds and an in room safe just as in the others.
